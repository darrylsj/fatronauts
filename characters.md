# Fatronauts - Character Bible

## Main Character (Unnamed - TBD)

**Basic Info:**
- Age: 28 years old
- Background: Black mother, unknown father (possibly white or other - never in his life)
- Grew up without a dad

**Day Job:**
- Car mechanic
- Regular working-class life

**Secret Identity: "Rocket Man"**
- Obsessed with rockets
- Posts anonymously in multiple Discord channels
- Sells rocket equations
- Provides answers and expertise to rocket enthusiasts worldwide
- Well-known in the Discord rocket community, but no one knows his real identity

**Personality:**
- The "bad guy" (antagonist energy, morally gray?)
- Self-doubt - doesn't think he belongs in formal spaces
- Doesn't see himself as qualified despite his genius

**The Inciting Incident:**
- Nationwide search across America for someone who knows rockets
- He thinks he shouldn't apply - impostor syndrome
- His friend **Eric** keeps poking him: "Dude, what are you doing? Apply!"
- Eric secretly submits the application on his behalf
- He receives a request from **NASA** to join
- This kicks off the story

## Sergei Volkov (Russian Character)

**Basic Info:**
- Russian cosmonaut
- Age: 40s
- The Original Survivor

**Background:**
- Wasn't supposed to be on the Mars mission
- Was accidentally left on the manifest
- Overweight, survived on Oreos and vodka
- **The ONLY crew member who survived radiation exposure**

**Significance:**
- His survival proved that body fat shields against deep-space radiation
- Became the reason NASA launched the "Fatronaut" program
- Living proof that unconventional heroes can save humanity

**Personality:**
- Vegetarian who ironically lives on Oreos and vodka
- Humble about his accidental heroism
- Has a dark sense of humor about being "too fat to die"

**Personality:**

- Takes action - submits the NASA application secretly
